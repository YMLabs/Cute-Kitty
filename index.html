Cute Kitty is a token devoted to the prevention of animal cruelty.

It is created on a DECENTRALISED BLOCKCHAIN PLATFORM. Every year thousands of animals suffer from neglect, cruelty and abuse. By purchasing the Cute Kitty token a donation will be made to a animal welfare organization or no kill animal shelter.

How It Works

With your purchase of Cute Kitty tokens there will be a 1.5% fee donated to an organization concerned with the health, safety and psychological wellness of individual animals.
